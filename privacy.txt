School Hub Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10/29/23

1. Introduction

Welcome to School Hub! This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about the collection, use, and protection of your personal information when you access or use our website. School Hub respects your privacy and is committed to safeguarding the data we collect. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand how we manage your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Staff Information: School Hub collects and stores limited personal data of staff members for the purposes of website management and security. The information we collect includes:

Full Name
Email Address
Lunch Period Teacher in School
2.2 Google AdSense and Google reCAPTCHA Data: School Hub may utilize Google AdSense and Google reCAPTCHA services. These services may collect data as outlined in Google's respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies for more information.

2.3 Games and Web Proxies: School Hub hosts games and web proxies that may collect data during their usage. Please be aware that the data collected by these external resources is not related to School Hub, and their respective privacy policies and terms of use apply.

3. Use of Information

3.1 Staff Data: The staff information collected is used for the purpose of verifying and managing access to certain features of School Hub. We may also use this information to contact you regarding updates or important notifications.

3.2 Google AdSense and Google reCAPTCHA Data: The data collected by Google AdSense and Google reCAPTCHA is used for the operation of these services and is governed by Google's policies.

4. Cookies

School Hub does not use cookies for data collection. We respect your privacy and have designed our website to minimize the collection of personal information.

5. Data Security

School Hub takes data security seriously. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. Third-Party Services

School Hub may use third-party services or applications that have their own privacy policies. Please review those policies when interacting with external content or services hosted on our website.

7. Staff Applications

7.1 Collection of Information: If you submit a staff application to School Hub, we will collect your name, email address, and lunch period teacher in school. This information is used exclusively for staff application purposes.

7.2 Retention: We retain staff application data for a reasonable period. If your application is unsuccessful, we will securely delete your information. If you become a staff member, your data will be managed as outlined in this policy.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

School Hub reserves the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes through our website.
